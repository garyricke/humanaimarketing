Market research (2024–2026, sourced) to reset Human AI Marketing's rate card from
introductory to premium positioning. Three research sweeps: site builds/conversions,
marketing retainers, premium training memberships. Full source URLs inline.

## 4 · Discount mechanics used by premium education brands

1. **Application-based partial aid (Coursera model):** short written application → 50–90% off,
   never 100% — keeps skin in the game. Tiered markdowns fit the existing code system.
2. **Scholarship seats with a creative application exercise (School of Motion model):**
   self-selecting, brand-building; maps to the student-free promise.
3. **Founding-member pricing:** 20–40% off list, locked for life, hard deadline. No
   application needed; standard at launch (Every: $500 first year vs $625 list).
4. Also seen: purchasing-power-parity regional pricing (Josh Comeau, Frontend Masters);
   verified nonprofit 50% (SmarterX); cohort early-bird codes (Maven, 25%).
