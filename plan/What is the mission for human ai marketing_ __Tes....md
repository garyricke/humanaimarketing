Based on your conversation, the core mission of **Human AI Marketing** is to disrupt traditional web development and marketing agencies by blending **generative AI workflows** with a **human-managed "white glove" service**.  
Instead of forcing clients to use rigid content management systems (like standard Squarespace or WordPress) or wrestle with AI themselves, your company handles everything behind the scenes using high-speed AI tools, while human managers (specifically local interns) act as the face-to-face, premium point of contact.

## **The Three Strategic Pillars**

To achieve this mission, the business operates on a three-pronged approach:

### **1\. Hyper-Local AI Persona Hubs (The Batavia Model)**

* **The Goal:** Build automated, hyper-local community portals (starting with BataviaIL.co, then scaling to Geneva, St. Charles, and eventually all of Chicago).  
* **The Mechanism:** Scraping all local organizations (businesses, churches, parks, schools) and using AI to generate targeted landing pages sorted by community personas (e.g., Downtown Social, Family Explorer, Youth).  
* **Monetization:** Ongoing advertising revenue, charging businesses for AI chat update access, and deploying interns to shoot high-quality video/drone footage for premium profile pages.

### **2\. AI-Driven B2B Agency Services (The "Uncle Tad" / Corporate Model)**

* **The Goal:** Win high-value corporate contracts by drastically undercutting traditional agency timelines and costs while maintaining a massive profit margin.  
* **The Mechanism:** Using deep AI competitive analysis to estimate what competitors spent on their digital footprints, then quoting an optimized price that saves the client money but reflects elite value.  
* **The Tech Stack:** Moving away from databases to build lightning-fast, ultra-secure, static front-end websites hosted on platforms like Netlify, paired with automated email marketing campaigns.

### **3\. The "White Glove" Intern Management Model**

* **The Goal:** Bridge the gap between advanced technology and human comfort.  
* **The Mechanism:** Clients never touch a line of code or an AI prompt. They simply hand off their content, emails, or updates to an intern. The intern utilizes the agency's internal AI toolkit (Claude, Gemini, OpenAI, Cloudinary) to execute changes in seconds. This provides a high-end service to the business owner and real-world tech experience to the intern.
