I Manifesto

The future of marketing isn't humans replaced by AI. It's humans amplified by AI, on behalf of other humans.

Human

The face.

Every client has a named contact — a local intern or manager you'll see at the Chamber lunch or Limestone Coffee. They answer the phone. They sign the email. They show up.

  • White-glove handoff — no CMS, no prompts, no dashboards
  • One real person, by name, for every account
  • Apprentice-built — interns get the byline, the credit, the experience
AI

The engine.

Behind the scenes, a full toolkit of generative models, scrapers, and pipelines produces work at a pace traditional agencies can't match — without ever appearing on the client's screen.

  • Claude · Gemini · OpenAI · Cloudinary · Nano Banana
  • Static front-ends shipped via GitHub → Netlify
  • Hyper-local AI persona hubs scaling Fox Valley → Chicago
Brand Promise

You talk. We build. In days, not quarters.

Rule 01

Hearth Orange and Signal Cyan rarely share equal weight. One leads, one accents.

Rule 02

Never pure black. Use Ink Navy. The warmth matters.

Rule 03

Bone is the default canvas. Pure White is for high-contrast moments only.

Rule 04

Maintain WCAG AA contrast — 4.5:1 minimum for body text.

VII Values
  1. 01

    Human First

    Every client has a named human contact. AI never answers a client email unsupervised.

  2. 02

    Hometown Loyalty

    We prioritize businesses, schools, and civic groups in our service area before chasing distant accounts.

  3. 03

    Light Speed

    If a deliverable takes a competitor six weeks, our target is six days. Speed is a feature.

  4. 04

    Honest Pricing

    We quote based on real AI compute plus real human hours. No agency tax. Margins come from efficiency, not opacity.

  5. 05

    Apprentice-Built

    Interns aren't a discount — they're the future of the industry. We train, mentor, and credit them publicly.

  6. 06

    Quietly Powerful Tech

    The client should never see the gears. The work should look effortless.
