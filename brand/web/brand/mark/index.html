h·AI IDENTITY

the mark.

An imperfect circle, drawn once. A human signal running through it.

01

what it is.

Two strokes, nothing else. Both drawn by hand, and both mean something.

The circle
An ensō — drawn in one motion, deliberately imperfect. It is the community we work inside, and the beginner's mind we bring to it. It never gets corrected into a perfect circle.
The wave
A human signal — a hand-drawn tilde riding through the middle. It is a voice, a heartbeat, a wavelength. The asymmetry is the point: no two passes of a pen are the same.
Together
The human signal inside the loop. Everything the agency makes should feel like this: technology holding the shape, a person supplying the life.

Clear space. Keep at least one quarter of the mark's diameter clear on every side — no type, no photos, no edges. The mark is quiet; give it room to be quiet in.

02

drawn three times.

One drawing cannot survive every size — thin hand-drawn strokes disappear at favicon scale. So the mark exists as three drawings with the same gesture: strokes thicken and the wobble calms down as the mark gets smaller. Pick by final rendered size, never by scaling whichever file is nearby.

03

with the name.

The wordmark is Fraunces, lowercase, with the AI set italic in teal. The mark sits to its left at cap-height-and-a-half, or above it when centered. On its own, the mark is reserved for places the name already lives — avatars, favicons, watermarks.

04

in color.

The palette is small on purpose: warm paper, ink, one quiet teal. The mark is Ink on the light surfaces and Bone on the dark ones. Teal is a rare accent — never the default. No orange, no bright cyan, no gradients, no shadows.

06

leave it alone.

The imperfection is the signature. Most damage comes from trying to help.

07

the files.

The two "Logo" files carry the mark plus the full name — use those for most materials. The "Mark" files are the circle alone. PNGs are transparent and ready to place; the SVGs scale losslessly for design tools.
