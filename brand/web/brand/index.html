02

the mark.

An ensō drawn in one motion, with a human signal running through it. It ships as three size-optimized drawings — full anatomy, usage mocks, and downloadable files live on the identity page.

The circle
Deliberately imperfect — the community we work inside and the beginner's mind we bring to it. Never corrected into a perfect circle.
The wave
A hand-drawn tilde — a voice, a heartbeat, a wavelength. The asymmetry is the point.
Clear space
One quarter of the mark's diameter on every side. No type, no photos, no edges.

04

the palette.

Small on purpose: two warm off-whites, ink, one quiet teal, and a deep sumi for emphasis. No orange, no bright cyan, no gradients, no drop shadows. When in doubt: more of the base color, less of everything else.

Base and lift

Pick one per page and hold it: Paper-as-base with Bone lifts (long reads, the handbook), or Bone-as-base with Paper lifts (marketing, photography-led pages). Never alternate within one document.

Teal is earned

Teal carries the AI in the wordmark and one accent word per headline — italic, never two. At display sizes over busy backgrounds, step down to teal-deep #355864.

HANDCaveat

— a note in the margin, used sparingly.

  • Headlines: lowercase or sentence case, ending with a period. Never all-caps except short labels (≤ 4 words) in supporting roles.
  • Headlines are bold by default (600); inside a paragraph, emphasize with italic, not bold.
  • One italic teal accent word per headline — never two.
  • Line height tightens as size grows: 1.6 body, 1.1 headings, ~1.04 display.

06

the surface.

Every page is paper. A barely-there speckle gives the surface tooth — felt more than seen. This page is wearing it at the official strength.

Bone + speckle — dot opacities 0.013 / 0.008 on a 3px / 7px grid. If you can point at the texture from arm's length, it's too strong.
Paper + speckle — same recipe on the warmer base. Generous margins, one idea per screen, whitespace is content.

07

the photographs.

Photography is the most beautiful thing on the page; the interface gets out of the way.

SubjectReal people in real rooms; real paper on real desks; real Fox Valley light.
TreatmentMinimal post-processing, slightly warm white balance, occasional film grain.
CompositionAsymmetry welcome; breathing room mandatory.
AvoidStock photography, AI-rendered humans, futuristic gradients, tech-aesthetic glow.

08

the voice.

Plainspoken, confident, specific, warm. A trusted neighbor who happens to have a computer science degree — never a pitch deck.

✓ on brand

"We rebuilt your site over the weekend. Here's the before and after."

"A real person in Batavia reads your note and writes back within one business day."

"Squarespace was fine in 2016. It's costing you money now."

✗ off brand

"We leverage cutting-edge AI to revolutionize your digital presence."

"Our world-class team of experts is standing by 24/7."

"Unlock synergistic ROI with our omnichannel growth solutions."
